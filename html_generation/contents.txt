Cultivated, ex Waimauku.%This monograph is a work in progress, documenting the growth stages of the small native orchids that can be found growing naturally in the locality where I live, including on my own section. Specific orchid species can be most readily identified by their flower. However, with carefull observation, the structure and form at each stage of growth is also unique to many of the species. The goal of this multi-season project is to record the necessary, sometimes short-lived, fine detail.<br><br>To find out more, click on a species name in the list below.
