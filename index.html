The Developmental Morphology of
New Zealand Native Orchids
by John Rugis

This illustrated monograph is a work in progress, documenting the growth stages of the diminutive native orchids that can be found growing naturally in the region where I live, including on my own property. Specific individual orchid species can be most readily identified by their flowers. However, with carefull observation, the structure and form at each stage of growth can be seen as unique to many of the species. The goal of this multi-season project is to document the necessary illustrative, sometimes short-lived, fine detail.


Species list:
   Acianthus sinclairii
   Caladenia chlorostyla
   Corybas trilobus
   Orthoceras novae-zeelandiae
   Pterostylis banksii
   Pterostylis trullifolia
   Thelymitra longifolia
   Thelymitra pulchella
